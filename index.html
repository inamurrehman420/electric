Royal Electrical

Royal Electrical is a local company providing electrical services with fully qualified electricians to Reading, Berkshire and the surrounding areas. We offer a professional service from start to finish at a reasonable rate; with industry experience our service is reliable and friendly and aims to be of the highest quality. Royal Electrical electricians aim to please new and existing customers with our high standards, experience, and knowledge. Royal Electrical work in both domestic and commercial settings, and can provide advice as well as work to time schedules alongside other trades. Using the latest industry principles and the newest technology Royal Electrical is able to offer competitive quotes to all customers, saving clients both time and money. We use the latest innovations and industry ideas in order to enhance the clients experience and provide a level of service that you may not expect from a local company.
